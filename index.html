Biography

    I am currently a PhD candidate at the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE), Beihang University. Besides, I am a research intern at Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, Tsinghua University, where I am a core developer of the Jittor framework. Additionally, I have served as a reviewer for academic journals like IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, Computational Visual Media Journal, etc.
